## Overview
This game is a simulation genre game designed to appeal to individuals of all ages. The primary goal is to prepare the desired products for customers visiting the café within a designated time frame, increasing the game score and difficulty level as orders are successfully completed.

## Game Concept
The game is set in a visually appealing coffee shop environment. Players take on the role of a barista, preparing and delivering orders to customers. The main objectives are to:
- Prepare and deliver customer orders accurately and swiftly.
- Increase the game score by fulfilling orders.
- Manage time effectively to handle increasing difficulty levels.

## Game Mechanics
### Gameplay
- Players control the barista using the computer mouse.
- The barista interacts with various interfaces within the coffee shop, including the coffee machine, espresso machine, ice machine, and dessert fridge.
- Orders are prepared and added to the inventory, then placed on a tray for delivery to the customer.

### Goal and Difficulty
- The objective is to prepare as many orders as possible within the given timeframe.
- The difficulty level increases as the player becomes more proficient in preparing orders quickly and accurately.

### Specific Mechanics
- Coffee preparation involves interacting with different machines and interfaces, each with its own preparation steps.
- Desserts are retrieved from the dessert fridge and added to the inventory for delivery.
